We are Manzini!

Manzini (which means "the waters" in the Zulu language) is a Car Wash and Car Care Center situated in various area in Soweto.

We have branches in the following areas:

  • place Mofolo Central
  • place Whitecity Jabavu
  • place Diepkloof
  • place Ennerdale
  • place Emmarentia

Our facilities all offer 5 Star Sevice with access to a restaurent and a relaxing lounge area. All clients can order a nice beverage whilst waiting for your car.
Visit our car wash today so that we can restore your ride to its former glory.

Remember always, we are the best mo Kasi!
